The Pyrolysis Process

Thermochemical decomposition of materials by heating without using oxygen, producing gas, oil, and solids.s

1

Collection

Mixed plastic waste is sorted and shredded into uniform pieces.

2

Heating

Plastic is heated in an oxygen-free chamber to prevent combustion.

3

Vaporization

Polymers break down into gas molecules through thermal cracking.

4

Condensation

Gases are cooled into liquid synthetic oil.
